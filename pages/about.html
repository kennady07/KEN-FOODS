ABOUT US





No dreamer is ever too small, no dream ever too big. KEN FOODS has come a long way. There are older restaurants and places with cooler backstories maybe, but for nearly 20 plus years, everyone in Chennai and beyond, young and old, is familiar with ‘KEN FOODS’. The early days were tougher times, but Mr.kennen single-handedly made ‘Empire’ possible and together with his family he ran the business with grit and fervour.



Exotic food like biryani and chickens were served, and also affordable price. They consistently built rapport, making each interaction with customers unique; such was their passion, a sure winner all the way.




OUR VISION



At KEN FOODS, we only strive to provide the best customer service possible while creating a happy memory of their time spent with us.




OUR MISSION



KEN FOODS wants to be your first choice for hosting a memorable event, whether it be a fun-filled eating experience or catering to one of your special occasions or events.
